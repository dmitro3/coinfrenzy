The Playbook

Win on socials — without the guesswork

Stuck on what to post, what to say, or which path to pick? Steal what's already working from creators who win with us. Skim it once, then start.

1 The 5-Move Formula

If you do nothing else, do these. Everything else on this page is just detail on how to nail each move.

1
Pick ONE path Personal brand, live stream, or faceless content. Stick with it for 14 days before adding another.
2
Post daily — short and entertaining 1–2 short videos a day. Hook in the first 1–2 seconds. Don't explain the platform — entertain.
3
Talk to people who engage Reply to every comment. DM people who show interest. Be casual — never pitch the platform.
4
Your bio link does the converting Drop your referral link in your bio. Mention "link in bio" naturally — only when someone asks, never as a hard sell.
5
Check Earnings weekly. Double down on winners. Look at what posts and what campaigns convert. Make more of that. Cut what doesn't work — without overthinking it.

What to post (pick 3 formats)

  • Big-win reactions and bonus highlights
  • Funny fails and bad-beat moments

  • Blackjack reactions ("the dealer really did me…")

Editing rules that work

  • Keep videos under 20 seconds
  • Captions on every video — 80% watch without sound
  • Hook in the first second (text or visual surprise)
  • Use trending audio — end before they expect

Bio formula

  • Line 1: what you post · Line 2: a hint of personality · Line 3: CTA + your link
  • Examples: "CoinFrenzy clips daily | Link below 👇" · "Sweepstakes moments that hit different | Play free 👇"
Kick (preferred) 4 streams/week · 60–120 min 5–15 clips per stream

Why this scales

  • One stream = 10+ short clips you can post to TikTok and Reels
  • Live chat reactions become content. Your real reactions read as authentic.
  • Re-purpose: 2–3 clips same day, the rest spread over 2–3 days

Stream rundown (90 min)

  • Warm-up (10 min): greet, recap last stream, set goals, drop a question
  • Main (60–70 min): commentary, interact with chat, mark "CLIP THAT" on big moments
  • Wrap (10–15 min): recap highlights, thank chat, tease next stream — natural CTA: "DM me 'INFO' for partner resources"

Pinned chat / verbal CTA (don't sell — invite)

  • Pinned: "Want to learn more? DM me 'INFO' after stream for partner resources."
  • Verbal: "Link's in my bio if you want to check it out — no pressure."
No face / no voice 2–4 posts/day Pick one page style

Page styles (pick one and master it)

  • Highlight page: daily wins and bonus moments
  • Meme / reaction page: POV captions over gameplay clips
  • Fails + wins page: the "you won't believe" energy

Format rules

  • Single clip: 8–15s · Compilation: 3–5 clips × 3–5s each (15–25s total)
  • POV format: text on screen 1–2s, then 5–10s of gameplay
  • Logo watermark on every clip — small (10–15% of frame), 70–80% opacity, same corner every time

Critical safety

  • Only use clips you own, license, or have approval for. No stolen content.
  • Don't scale to a second page until you're consistently above ~1K views/post and the workflow takes under an hour a day

5 Your First 7 Days

A simple kickoff plan that gets you posting fast. Don't overthink it — momentum matters more than perfection.

DAY 1
Set up. Pick your path. Update your bio with your referral link. Create your first campaign on the Campaigns tab. Post your first short video — even if it's not perfect.
DAY 2–3
Get reps in. 1–2 short videos per day. Try 2 different hook styles from the toolkit. Reply to every comment.
DAY 4–5
Start DMing. Pick 5 people who engaged with your stuff. Use the cold openers from the toolkit. Be casual — let them ask.
DAY 6
Spot a winner. Look at your views. Note your best post. Make 2 more videos in that exact format.
DAY 7
Check in. Open the Earnings tab. Look at signups. Plan next week: more of what worked, drop what didn't. Repeat.

Real talk: the people who win here aren't always the ones with the most followers. They're the ones who post consistently for weeks, not days. If something feels off, hit the Rules & FAQs tab — most questions are answered there.

6 FAQs

Tap any question to expand.

How is my commission calculated?

You earn a percentage of the platform's Net Gaming Revenue (NGR) from the players you refer. In plain English: when your players win on net, the platform absorbs the loss and your commission for that period is $0 — you never owe anything. When your players lose on net, you earn your share of what's left after we cover provider and processing fees.

Your rate is set on your account (default 10%) and your "Estimated Earnings" tile refreshes every day from the latest CoinFrenzy activity. The number is finalized when payouts are processed each week. If your network is in a temporary down-swing the tile will say Building — that just means we're waiting on the next round of purchases to swing positive. Nothing is ever clawed back from you.

How and when do I get paid?

Payouts are processed weekly by our team. Earnings are pushed to your Lightning Bolt on CoinFrenzy — the same lightning icon at the top of the site where you collect bonuses — and you simply claim them there. You'll see Pending, Processing, and Paid states on your Earnings page. If you exceed annual reporting thresholds (currently $600 in the U.S.), we'll issue you a 1099 at year-end.

What does my promo code do?

Each campaign you create has a unique promo code and link. Anyone who signs up with your code or via your link is permanently attributed to you. Codes are also a fallback when someone forgets to use your link.

Can I have multiple campaigns?

Yes — unlimited. Create one per platform (TikTok, Instagram, Kick, etc.) so you can see which channel actually drives signups. Each campaign has its own promo code, so signups are attributed to the exact campaign someone used.

What does each player status mean?

Signed up — created an account but hasn't made a purchase yet. Purchased — has made at least one purchase on CoinFrenzy. VIP — has $500 or more in lifetime purchases. Only purchasing players generate revenue you can earn from.

Should I mention I'm a partner?

Yes — when you're promoting your link or code, just be transparent that you're a CoinFrenzy partner and you earn a commission on signups. Something like "#ad — partner with CoinFrenzy" in your caption or bio works perfectly.

Can I run paid ads on Google or social platforms?

Not without written approval from CoinFrenzy. Paid ads on Google, Meta, TikTok, X, and similar networks are restricted to protect our brand and avoid policy conflicts. Organic content (your own posts, videos, streams, communities) is always welcome.

What if I slip up?

For small stuff we'll just give you a heads-up and a chance to update the content. The serious things (income guarantees, targeting minors, promoting in restricted states, paid ads without approval, impersonating CoinFrenzy) can mean account suspension. Pretty rare — most partners never have an issue.

What states are restricted?

The full current list is in the "Restricted States" block above. Don't actively target audiences in these states. The list is subject to change as state laws evolve — when in doubt, ask before posting.

Who do I contact for help?

Email coinfrenzyteam@gmail.com for any partner-related question — payouts, attribution, account access, or content review. Replies are typically within 1 business day.
